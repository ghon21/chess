Is this better than Chess.com's built-in analysis? +
Chess.com shows you what happened. GrandmasterAI tells you WHY it keeps happening and builds a personalized training plan to fix it permanently. We track patterns across all your games, not just one at a time.
Do I need to be a strong player to benefit? +
Not at all — our biggest gains come from 400–1400 ELO players who have consistent blind spots. The AI adapts its explanations and drill difficulty entirely to your current level. Beginners and advanced players both see dramatic results.
How does it connect to my existing games? +
One-click import from Chess.com and Lichess with username sync, or paste any PGN directly into the analyzer. FIDE rated game archives are also supported. Your entire game history can be bulk-imported in minutes.
What engine does it use? +
Stockfish 16 running at depth 26, paired with our proprietary explanation layer trained on 10 million+ annotated Grandmaster games. You get world-class engine accuracy with human-readable context for every variation.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — no contracts, no penalties. Cancel directly from your dashboard in 2 clicks. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation, and you can export everything at any time.
Is my game data private? +
All analyses are private by default. We never share your games, opening preparation, or analysis reports with other users or third parties without your explicit consent. Full GDPR compliance and data export available.
